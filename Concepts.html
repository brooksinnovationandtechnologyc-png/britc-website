BRITC Rose SUV

Automotive Technology Concept

Stage:Concept Development
  • 65 kWh lithium iron phosphate battery providing 200+ mile electric-only range with 31-gallon fuel tank for 400+ mile total range
  • Dual-track powered sliding doors requiring only 12 inches lateral clearance (vs 36+ inches for swing doors) with obstacle detection and soft-close
  • Quad-motor electric drive (60 kW per wheel) with permanent all-wheel drive, regenerative braking, and individual wheel torque vectoring
  • 2-2-3 seating for seven adults with 14-inch walk-through center aisle to third row and 60/40 split-folding rear seats
  • Four-wheel steering (front ±40°, rear ±15°) with AI-assisted precision parking achieving ±3 cm positioning accuracy below 2 mph
  • Hierarchical AI control: ISO 26262 ASIL-D certified safety kernel with secondary AI optimization layer and <100ms human override latency
  • 1.5L turbocharged inline-3 range extender operating at fixed 3,000 RPM driving 50 kW generator for battery recharging with minimal noise
  • Octovalve integrated thermal management system with seven-zone cabin climate control and battery thermal conditioning for optimal charging
  • Structural battery integration with crash safety targets: NHTSA 5-star overall, IIHS Top Safety Pick+, equivalent side-impact protection to swing doors
  • 150 kW DC fast charging capability and 7.2 kW vehicle-to-load bidirectional power for tools, camping, or emergency backup power

Requires automotive engineering development, crash safety validation (NHTSA, IIHS), emissions certification (EPA, CARB), battery safety testing (UN ECE R100), manufacturing partnership, and regulatory approval for production
