About BRITC

American Innovation. Christian Values. Technical Excellence.

Who We Are

Brooks Innovation and Technology Consulting LLC (BRITC) is a Christian technology consulting company that aims to create designs for technology in industries such as defense, energy, agriculture, and transportation. We also aim to produce software including video games and budgeting applications.

Founded in the State of Hawaii on November 21, 2025, BRITC was established with the aim of producing capable and efficient technology for the United States. Our mission is to serve our nation through technological innovation while upholding our Christian principles of integrity, excellence, and service.

Our Business Model

BRITC carries out projects that result in two primary deliverables:

1. Finished Software Applications

Complete, production-ready software solutions including enterprise applications, specialized tools, and consumer software products.

2. Design Packages

Comprehensive digital folders containing software, technical documentation, CAD files, and all necessary resources that enable clients to produce the technology detailed in the package from scratch. These packages are either:

  • Leased to other companies for construction with royalty payments on each unit constructed (not on each sale)
  • Produced internally through a subsidiary or associated company

American-First Commitment

BRITC is committed to strengthening American technological independence. We prioritize:

  • American Natural Resources: Designs optimized for domestic material availability
  • American Labor: Legal American workforce for all development and production
  • American Markets: U.S. government and commercial customers receive priority
  • Domestic Supply Chains: Reducing foreign dependencies in critical technologies

Our Christian Foundation

As a Christian technology firm, BRITC operates according to biblical principles:

  • Integrity: Honest dealing in all business relationships
  • Excellence: Pursuing the highest quality in our work
  • Stewardship: Responsible use of resources and talents
  • Service: Contributing to the common good and national security
  • Community: Building a workplace culture that honors faith and family

We welcome employees and partners who share our values and commitment to excellence.
